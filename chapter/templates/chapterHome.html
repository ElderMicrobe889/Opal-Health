BASH TSA

What is TSA?

The Technology Student Association (TSA), is a club, otherwise known as a FLEX at some schools. In TSA, students work individually or in groups in different STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) events.

TSA at BASH

Boyertown Area Senior High, BASH, is one of the best schools in the area. There are many extra-curricular activities offered here, one of them being TSA. TSA has been running at BASH for around 31 years now, and is still going strong.

BCTC

Berks Career and Technology Center (BCTC) is a program that offers students opportunities to gain real-world experience in the field of their choice. Students go off-campus to the BCTC campuses in order to gain the skills and certifications needed to work in the real world.
