Step 1 - Sign Up

Go to the Sign Up page, and create an account. You’ll get a confirmation email afterwards, confirm your email and move on to step 2.

Step 2 - Schedule A Meeting

Click the “Schedule an appointment” link on the Appointments page and fill in the required information. When one of our doctors accepts the appointment, you’ll get an email reminding you of the appointment.

Step 3 - Join The Meeting

When it is time for the appointment, find it in the list shown on the Appointments page. Configure your microphone and camera settings and join the call to talk to one of our skilled doctors via the text chat or your microphone.
